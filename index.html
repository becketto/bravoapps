Affilitrak

Affiliate Marketing Made Simple

Affilitrak is a simple to use affiliate marketing app that allows you to easily onboard new affiliates, pay commissions automatically, and let affiliates manage their own programs. Affiliates can create their own links and coupon codes and Affilitrak seamlessly tracks referrals and commissions.

The app is made to be simple to learn and "just work". Features like tiers and automatic logic make the app hands off and convenient.

Custom Commission Logic

Create custom complex commission logic or use simple flat commissions that work for your business model

Smart Tracking

Track referrals through coupons or links and let affiliates make their own codes automatically

Automated Payments

Pay affiliates automatically with PayPal or manually log payments with full transaction history

Multi-Level Marketing

Setup a custom multi-level marketing program with advanced tier structures and rewards

Background Automation

Automate processes so your affiliate program can run in the background without constant management

Easy Onboarding

Simple affiliate onboarding process that gets your partners selling quickly and efficiently
